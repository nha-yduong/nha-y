Concert Works

As a composer, my work spans both the world of video game music and concert music, allowing me to explore a wide range of musical styles and genres. In addition to creating dynamic soundtracks for video games, I have composed for various concert music ensembles, showcasing my versatility as a composer.

My concert music compositions include art songs, where I blend vocal and instrumental elements to create expressive and evocative works. I've written for string quartet, crafting intricate pieces that highlight the beauty and depth of the string ensemble, and I’ve also composed other chamber music, exploring different instrumental combinations to create intimate and emotive works. My piano compositions explore a wide range of styles, from classical to contemporary, and provide an opportunity to experiment with different textures and emotions. Currently, I’m working on a wind band piece, which presents a new and exciting challenge as I explore the unique sound world of wind instruments.

These diverse experiences in concert music continue to shape my growth as a composer, and I’m excited to continue pushing the boundaries of my creativity in both video game music and concert composition.
