About Me

Hello! I’m Nha-Y, a music composer and pianist with a passion for crafting evocative soundscapes that bring stories, emotions, and ideas to life. My work spans from video games to chamber pieces to wind band. The piano has been my creative anchor since the beginning of my musical journey. It’s where I first discovered the magic of turning emotions into melodies, and it remains at the heart of my compositions. Whether it’s a delicate solo piece or a foundation for a larger arrangement, the piano allows me to explore depth and nuance in every note. I believe music is a powerful storytelling tool, capable of expressing what words cannot. Setting the mood for a cinematic moment, enhancing the immersion of a game world, or composing standalone pieces, I strive to connect deeply with my audience through every note.Let’s create something unforgettable together. Feel free to explore my work, and don’t hesitate to reach out for collaborations or inquiries.

Concert Works

Concert music has always held a special place in my heart—it’s where my musical journey truly began. Long before I discovered the joy of composing for video games, I fell in love with the beauty and depth of concert music. Its ability to evoke raw emotion, tell profound stories, and connect with audiences on a deeply human level continues to inspire me every day. I hope you will take a look and enjoy the various pieces I have composed!
