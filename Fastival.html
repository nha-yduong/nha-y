Fastival

Click here to download on Steam!

In Fastival, I had the opportunity to compose the main menu theme, setting the tone for the entire game. Inspired by the vibrant and whimsical circus vibe of the game, I crafted a chill jazz piece that combines a playful yet soothing melody using piano, drums, and a walking bass line. The theme is designed to immerse players in the lively yet relaxed atmosphere of the game world, evoking a sense of wonder and excitement while maintaining a laid-back charm. This composition serves as the perfect introduction to the unique and captivating experience that Fastival offers.
